Personal Information

I am a freshman studying Computer Science at The George Washington University, class of 2021. Some of my hobbies include reading, hiking, travelling, and photography. Learn more by clicking each hobby, or view some of my pictures in the "Photography" tab.

Before GW

I am originally from Richmond, VA, and I graduated from Monacan High School in June of 2017. Before I moved to D.C. I volunteered at an animal rescue group by taking care of rescued dogs. I also worked at Mathnasium, where I taught and tutored students in Kindergarten through 8th grade different math concepts.

Books

In general, I really like 20th century novels, but my absolute favorite author is Pat Conroy, my favorite of his books being The Lords of Discipline. Other authors I like include George Orwell and John Updike.

Hikes

Since I am from a city, I haven't been on a ton of exciting hiking trips. However, my favorite place to go is Shenandoah National Park because of the beautiful views like the one shown above. Rose River and Whiteoak Canyon trails are some of the best hikes at Shenandoah if you're able to make it all the way to the top!

Travel

I love to travel, and I would love to travel to as many different places as possible in the future. I've been to Iceland, Quebec, and Ireland, as well as many different states. Iceland was my favorite because of its remoteness and natural beauty, despite the freezing temperatures!
